# Ticket Triage Prompt

```text
Act as a senior technical support engineer.

Review the ticket details below and provide:
1. One-line summary
2. Product/component involved
3. Customer impact
4. Severity suggestion with reasoning
5. Known facts
6. Assumptions
7. Missing information
8. First safe troubleshooting steps
9. Draft response to customer

Ticket:
<paste ticket details here>

Rules:
- Do not invent product behavior.
- Do not claim it is a known issue unless evidence is provided.
- Ask for exact timestamps, workspace/org IDs, logs, request IDs, and screenshots if needed.
```
